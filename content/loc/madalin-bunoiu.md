Madalin Bunoiu has a distinguished career in higher education and scientific
research, with extensive experience in quality assurance, academic strategy,
and scientific innovation.

Currently serving as Pro-Rector at the West University of Timișoara (UVT), he
oversees the institution's research, development, and innovation strategy,
technology transfer, sustainability efforts, and relations with the diaspora.
His academic and administrative career spans over two decades, with prior roles
as Pro-Rector for Academic Strategy and Student Relations and Chancellor of the
Faculty of Physics at UVT.

He is a physicist, specialized in materials science, crystal growth and
characterization, and educational physics. Since 2014, he was a member of the
Council of the Romanian Agency for Quality Assurance in Higher Education
(ARACIS), and from January 2022 to December 2023 he was the President of
ARACIS.

For his research activities, Madalin Bunoiu was rewarded, in 2012, with the
"Constantin Miculescu" prize of the Romanian Academy, Physics Section. For his
entire activity he was rewarded, in 2019, with the "Les Palmes Academiques"
order of the French Republic and, in 2023, as a sign of high appreciation for
the special contribution made to the promotion of Romania's image among the
member states of the International Organization of Francophonie, the President
of Romania conferred him the National Order "Faithful Service" in the rank of
Knight.
