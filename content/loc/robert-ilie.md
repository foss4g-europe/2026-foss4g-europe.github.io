Robert Ille is a surveying and geomatics professional based in Romania, with practical experience in topography, geodesy, cadastre, and modern spatial data acquisition technologies. He is the founder of Digital Mapping, a company focused on delivering real-world geospatial solutions and technical support for field professionals.

Robert works extensively with GNSS, total stations, and 3D LiDAR SLAM systems, supporting workflows for as-built documentation, 3D modeling, and land surveying. Digital Mapping is also the official TopoGEOS distributor in Romania, helping bring advanced surveying technologies closer to local practitioners.

Actively involved in field projects and technology demonstrations, Robert is passionate about turning cutting-edge tools into everyday working solutions.
