Registration fee includes:

- [**FOSS4G Europe 2026 Conference**](/schedule/talks/) (29 June - 1 July 2026)  
Access to 3 conference days including the opening plenary session, conference
tracks, coffee breaks, and lunch.

- [**Ice-Breaker**](/schedule/ice-breaker/) (29 June 2026)  
The Ice-Breaker event is included in the conference ticket for all registered
participants.

- [**Workshops**](/schedule/workshops/) (2 - 3 July 2026)  
Workshops are organized in 4-hour and 2-hour sessions. Participants are invited to use
their own computer.

- [**FOSS4G Europe 2026 Community Sprint**](/schedule/community-sprint/) (4 - 5 July 2026)  
The Community Sprint is planned as a complimentary event.

## Schedule
A more in-depth overview of the schedule and various events taking place during
the conference can be found under [**Schedule**](../schedule/).

## Tickets and prices

Please note that FOSS4GE workshops can only be bought as an add-on to the main
conference ticket. Workshops can only be attended by participants owning a main
conference ticket.

### Conference ticket

- `FOSS4GE Conference - Early bird`: 1.300,00 RON (approx. 254.90 EUR)
- `FOSS4GE Conference - Regular`: 300.00 EUR
- `FOSS4GE Conference - On-site`: 350.00 EUR

This ticket includes:

- Access to 3 conference days
- Ice-Breaker on 29 June
- Daily lunch
- Coffee breaks

Early bird tickets will be available until 1 May 2026.

### Workshops

- 4-hour workshop: 182,00 RON (approx. 35.69 EUR)
- 2-hour workshop: 91,00 RON (approx. 17.84 EUR)

### Social and special events

- [**GeoChicas**](/schedule/geochicas-timisoara/): free
- [**B2B - 29 June**](/schedule/b2b/): 385,00 RON (approx. 75.49 EUR)
- [**Gala Dinner**](/schedule/gala-dinner/): 442,00 RON (approx. 86.67 EUR)
- `Adopt-a-ticket`: 1.560,00 RON (approx. 305.88 EUR)

### Additional products

- [**Recas winery visit**](/schedule/excursion/): 442,00 RON (approx. 86.67 EUR)
- [**Timisoara city guided tour**](/schedule/city-tour/): 36,40 RON (approx. 7.14 EUR)

#### CANCELLATION POLICY

The cancellation should be directed to **info-europe@foss4g.org** in writing
only. A 25% administrative charge will be held from the registration cost by
the organizers if the cancellation is announced before May 30, 2026. After this
date, no refund will be possible. Refunds will be processed and issued up to 60
days after the conference.

Unexpected cancellation of the event or impossibility to hold it.
In the unlikely case that the FOSS4GE 2026 cannot be held in whole or in part
due to any event beyond the Organisers’ control or *force majeure*, the
Conference Organisers cannot be held liable for any damage, cost, or loss
incurred, such as registration fee or any other direct or indirect loss or
consequential damage.
