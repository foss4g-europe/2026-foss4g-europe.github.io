## Recas Winery Trip

Location: Wineshop and Tours, Complex de Vinificatie CP1 Office, 307340 Recas,
[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Gi2nisyNT1gsN7gm6)

Recas Winery is one of Romania's most prominent and modern wine producers,
located in the city of Recas, Timis County, in the Banat region in western
Romania. Founded in the early 2000s, this winery has quickly established
itself as a leader in Romanian viticulture and winemaking.

Situated in the Banat wine region, Recas benefits from an ideal continental
climate with Pannonian influences, warm summers, mild winters, and
well-drained soils that provide excellent conditions for grape cultivation. The
winery's vineyards span several hundred hectares across gently rolling hills.

Recas produces an impressive range of wines, from crisp white varieties like
Sauvignon Blanc, Chardonnay, and Riesling, to robust reds including Cabernet
Sauvignon, Merlot, and Pinot Noir. They are particularly known for their
premium selections and limited editions that showcase the potential of Romanian
terroir.

Recas has gained international recognition, with its wines being exported to
numerous countries and receiving awards at prestigious wine competitions. The
winery has become a symbol of Romanian winemaking, combining innovation with
respect for local grape varieties and traditions.

## Guided tour, wine tasting, and dinner

The experience includes a guided tour, wine tasting, and traditional Romanian
cuisine.

We offer wine tasting paired with an abundant buffet featuring authentic
Romanian cuisine and international favorites, together with a carefully curated
selection of wines and artisanal accompaniments including fresh bread with
olive oil, assorted local cheeses, cured ham, and premium chocolate.

Buffet features for Wednesday evening include fresh salmon, oven-roasted
chicken, tender pork tenderloin, traditional beef goulash, succulent pork
ribs, layered polenta with cheese and sour cream, herb-roasted potatoes,
seasonal roasted vegetables, fresh cabbage salad, caprese salad with tomatoes,
mozzarella, and basil, apple, cheese, and poppy seed cake, hot peppers, fresh
sour cream, artisanal bread, and beverages including selected wines, water,
and freshly brewed coffee.

This culinary journey combines the rich flavors of Romanian traditional cooking
with modern gastronomy, perfectly complemented by wine selection and good
music.
