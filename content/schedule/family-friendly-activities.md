## Kids' Geocaching Adventure

Join us for a Kids’ Geocaching Adventure in the green areas along the Bega
River, right around the West University of Timișoara (WUT). Using GPS and
clues, kids will search for hidden "caches" tucked away in nature, crack codes,
and discover the basics of location-based exploration in a fun and interactive
way.

This hands-on activity is designed for curious minds who enjoy the outdoors,
problem-solving, and teamwork.
