# Volunteers

FOSS4G Europe 2026 is looking for enthusiastic volunteers. Applications are open until **April 30th**.
As part of the volunteer team, you will help keep the conference running smoothly while gaining a front-row
experience of the open geospatial world. You will collaborate with speakers, connect with passionate community
members, and enjoy access to talks and workshops for roughly half of the event whenever you are not on duty.

Volunteers commit to at least **20 hours** distributed across a minimum of **3 days** during the conference.
In return, the registration fee will be covered.

Those interested in collaboration as volunteers should submit the Google form provided in this Volunteers
section before **April 30th**. After the review process, selected candidates will be announced on **May 7th**.

Once selected, you will hear from the Volunteer Committee, sign an agreement, and join our Telegram
coordination group.

<br>

### Positions needed throughout the conference

**Preconference day**

Work in assembly-line style to prepare and organize items, programs, bags, badges, stickers, check conference
rooms, set directional signage, prepare the early registration desk, and related tasks.

**Conference room maintenance**

Stationed in one of the conference or workshop rooms to support presenters with presentation setup, troubleshoot
technical issues in the room, and provide general support and guidance to attendees.

**Registration desk and information**

Stationed at the registration desk to sign in attendees, distribute name tags and conference materials, and
answer attendee questions. Knowledge of the local area is a plus.

**Support at specific key locations**

Stationed at key locations throughout the conference venue to offer directions and answer attendee questions.
You may also be called to support other stations when extra backup is needed.
