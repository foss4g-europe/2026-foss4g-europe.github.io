Stefan holds a PhD in coastal geomorphology (2005), with a research focus on
the analysis of Romania's cliffed coasts through the integration of historical
maps, satellite imagery, and archival photography within geospatial databases.

He is a faculty member at the University of Bucharest, Faculty of Geography,
where he teaches Oceanography, Geography of the Black Sea, GIS, and Historical
Cartography.

Since 1996, he has been conducting extensive research on the Danube Delta at
the university's Marine and Fluvial Research Station from Sf. Gheorghe. His
expertise lies in applying GIS techniques to analyze deltaic shoreline
evolution.

Additionally, he is a co-founder of
[geo-spatial.org](https://geo-spatial.org/), an initiative promoting open
access to geospatial data.
