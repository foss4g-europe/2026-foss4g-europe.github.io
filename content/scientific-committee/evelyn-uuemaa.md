Evelyn is a geospatial scientist specialising in big geospatial data
management, data quality, and environmental modelling. Over the last five
years, her research has focused on machine learning-driven predictive spatial
modelling.

She is passionate about open-source tools and open data, and an avid
participant in the 30DayMapChallenge. She serves as a Professor in
Geoinformatics and leads the Landscape Geoinformatics Lab at the University of
Tartu.
