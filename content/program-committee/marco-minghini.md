Marco has been an OSGeo Charter Member since 2015. He currently works as a
Scientific Project Officer and Policy Analyst at the [https://joint-research-centre.ec.europa.eu](Joint Research Centre
(JRC)) of the European Commission in Ispra, Italy, providing scientific
evidence in support to policies on (geospatial) data sharing, data spaces,
open source and digital sovereignty. Among others, the team he is part of
coordinates the technical implementation and evolution of the [https://inspire.ec.europa.eu/](INSPIRE
Directive). He is a researcher, educator and advocate on open source software
and open data.

Since 2022 he is the Chair of [https://www2.isprs.org/commissions/comm4/icwg-4-3-2/](ISPRS Intercommission Working Group "Open
innovation in Geospatial science and Remote Sensing"). He is a regular
participant and speaker at Italian, European and global FOSS4G events. He was
the Secretary of [http://europe.foss4g.org/2015](FOSS4G Europe 2015), a member of the Local Organising Team of
[https://2022.foss4g.org/](FOSS4G 2022) and one of the Co-chairs of the Academic Track at [https://2022.foss4g.org/](FOSS4G 2022),
[https://2023.foss4g.org/](FOSS4G 2023), [https://2024.europe.foss4g.org/](FOSS4G Europe 2024) and [https://2025.europe.foss4g.org/](FOSS4G Europe 2025). Since 2019, at
European FOSS4G events he co-organises a dedicated Track on European
applications of free and open source software.
