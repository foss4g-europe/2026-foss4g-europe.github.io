Timișoara offers accommodation options for all types of budgets: from low cost hostels to premium hotels.
There is also the option of renting private accommodations using services like AirBnB and Booking.

## Accommodation options

### Hotel Timisoara

[Website](https://hoteltimisoara.ro/en/)

Price ranges from 80 to 120 euro/night, including breakfast, taxes, and access
to the SPA.

For FOSS4G Europe 2026 participants, a discount is available based on the code
`VEST2026`. This must be mentioned in the reservation request sent by e-mail to
[office@hoteltimisoara.ro](mailto:office@hoteltimisoara.ro). Rooms will be
reserved subject to availability.

Location: Str. Marasesti nr. 1-3, 300086, Timisoara,
[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/W8Kg4HxysWH8afST7)

### Hotel RIVO

[Website](https://hotelrivo.ro/)

This hotel is located in a quiet area near the People's Park, close to the
Bega River, and just a 15-minute walk from the city center and the West
University of Timisoara.

This hotel offers rooms with discount for the FOSS4G participants (price range
65-80 euros/night/single or double room, including breakfast, parking, and
other hotel facilities) using the discount code `FOSS4G` by sending the
reservation details and this code to
[office@brancogroup.ro](mailto:office@brancogroup.ro).

Rooms will be reserved subject to availability.

Location: Str. Johann Heinrich Pestalozzi 1/a, 300115, Timisoara,
[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/cBWo7pyyfGUBCdW19)

### IBIS Timisoara City Center

[Website](https://all.accor.com/booking/en/ibis/hotel/B3H6?destination=timisoara-romania&compositions=1&dateIn=2026-03-02&dateOut=2026-03-03&nights=1&hideWDR=false&accessibleRoom=false)

The hotel offers a discount based on the code `FOSS4G-IBIS` (meaning 64
euro/night single room and 75 euro/night double room). Those interested should
send an e-mail to [andreea.ancas@accor.com](mailto:andreea.ancas@accor.com),
mentioning the above code as a FOSS4G participant.

Rooms will be reserved subject to availability.

Location: Strada Coriolan Brediceanu 18, 300012, Timisoara,
[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/cjFjttN3aRmiyfJg6)

### Hotel DelCorso

[Website](https://hoteldelcorso.ro/en)

This hotel offers a 15% discount for FOSS4G participants. Those interested
should access [this booking link](https://hoteldelcorso.rooms-wizard.com/) and
use the code `FOSS4G`, or send an e-mail to
[reservation@hoteldelcorso.ro](mailto:reservation@hoteldelcorso.ro) referring
to the same discount code `FOSS4G`.

Rooms will be reserved subject to availability.

Location: Calea Bogdanestilor 18, Timisoara,
[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/c7Cmm7YUtCUmG4vS9)

### Hotel Atlas

[Website](https://atlashotel.ro/)

This hotel offers a 10% discount for the FOSS4G Europe 2026 participants. The
name of the event (`FOSS4G Europe 2026`) should be mentioned when a reservation
e-mail is sent through the link from the above website.

Location: Piata Sf. Gheorghe nr. 2-3, Timisoara 300085,
[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/wX3744oYH6HuA9Wf9)

### Hotel Galaxy Timisoara (Radisson Individuals)

This is a new hotel located in the central part of Timisoara, part of Radisson
Individuals, and it will open starting in April. To benefit from the discount,
participants will have to reserve their rooms by accessing the following link:

[Website](https://www.radissonhotels.com/en-us/hotels/radisson-individuals-galaxy-timisoara)

Location: 18-20 Gheorghe Lazar Street, Timisoara,
[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Sd8Yi34iuJpcAPQa7)

We are also considering providing a limited number of accommodation options in
student dormitories.

Student participants will be offered the option for accommodation in the
university facilities at a discounted rate.
