### Transport to Timișoara


---

#### By Air

The Timișoara International Airport offers direct flights to multiple European destinations.
Other nearby airports with transfer by car include Budapest and Belgrade.

---

#### By Rail

Regional and international trains are available, including international connections to Budapest and Vienna.

---

#### By Car

Timișoara is situated along the 4th Pan-European Corridor and is easily accessible from Hungary, Serbia and within Romania.

---

#### By Bus

International and regional bus connections are available.
