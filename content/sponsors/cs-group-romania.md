CS GROUP - ROMANIA, a proud affiliate of CS GROUP - France, has been shaping
the IT landscape since its establishment in 1991. As part of a global mid cap
technology leader headquartered in France, CS GROUP operates across Europe,
Asia, and North America, bringing together over 2,600 experts.

In March 2023, CS GROUP - and implicitly CS GROUP - ROMANIA - joined forces
with SOPRA STERIA GROUP. This strategic acquisition unites two high value
players to create a powerhouse in digital services and critical systems, with
strong capabilities across space, defense, security, and nuclear industries.
Together, we reinforce our leading position in aerospace and cybersecurity
markets.

## Our Organization

CS GROUP - ROMANIA employs 70 highly skilled professionals, including 60
engineers specializing in Computer Engineering, Control Systems, and
Informatics, holding Master's and PhD level expertise. Over 50 of our engineers
are dedicated to Aeronautics and Space programs.

## Our Expertise

CS GROUP - ROMANIA delivers high value engineering and digital solutions across
three major lines:

### Aeronautics and Space

We provide advanced software development, maintenance, and embedded systems
engineering aligned with industry standards such as DO 178B and ECSS E ST 40C.

Our teams have contributed to mission critical systems, including:

- Flight display and warning systems
- Digital engine control
- Cartography and satellite data processing
- Flight data processing and flight dynamics
- Engineering support and software V&V for space missions

We are proud to collaborate with leading global customers such as ESA, Airbus
Group, Thales Alenia Space, SAFRAN Group, and Pratt and Whitney.

### Energy

In the Energy sector, our solutions range from Command and Control Systems to
integrated digital platforms for:

- Hydropower management
- Plant level and enterprise level systems integration
- SCADA and automation software development and commissioning

Our key partners include Hidroelectrica, Andritz Hydro, Voith Hydro, and EDF.

### Innovation and European Projects

CS GROUP - ROMANIA is an active contributor to European research and
innovation, participating in FP7, Horizon 2020, and Horizon Europe initiatives.
Our projects focus on:

- Data enrichment
- Artificial Intelligence
- Earth Observation (EO) market development

Our impact has been recognized by the Joint Research Centre (JRC), which
identified us as a Key Innovator supporting resilient infrastructure,
sustainable industrialization, and innovation.
