Founded in 1996, virtual7 GmbH is a leading German IT consultancy and software
development firm specializing in digital transformation and data-driven
solutions. Headquartered in Karlsruhe, the company has grown into a key partner
for both public sector institutions and private enterprises across Germany and
Switzerland.

The company focuses on bridging the gap between complex IT infrastructures and
user-centric digital services. Their primary areas of operation include:

- Public sector digitalization: modernizing government processes and enabling
  e-government through agile methodologies
- Data and analytics: BI, data warehousing, and big data solutions that turn
  raw information into actionable insights
- Oracle specialist: long-standing Oracle Platinum Partner expertise including
  Oracle Middleware, database systems, and APEX
- Cloud and modern architecture: cloud migrations, microservices, and DevOps
  practices for scalable, future-proof IT landscapes

What makes virtual7 unique is their longevity in a fast-paced industry.
Having navigated the dot-com bubble and the shifts in cloud computing, they
offer a mix of stability and innovation. They are frequently recognized for
their "New Work" culture, fostering an environment of continuous learning and
flat hierarchies.

For over 25 years, virtual7 has maintained a significant presence in Romania,
specifically Timisoara, making them a notable bridge for IT talent between
Eastern and Central Europe.
