Consortis Geospatial is a geospatial technology and software company that
combines specialist geospatial services with proprietary and Commercial
Off-The-Shelf (COTS) software products for infrastructure organizations and
asset-intensive enterprises. Leveraging GIS, IoT, Earth Observation data,
mobile mapping, LiDAR, photogrammetry, digital twins, and AI/ML, the company
designs and delivers solutions for the collection, processing, analysis, and
visualization of spatial data, as well as for real-time infrastructure
monitoring and management.

The company develops early warning systems for natural phenomena, integrating
IoT sensors, satellite data, and machine learning (ML), while offering aerial
and mobile digital mapping services. Additionally, it provides management,
processing, and utilization services of satellite Earth observation data,
through innovative AI/ML methodologies for monitoring and management
applications of the anthropogenic and natural environment.

Beyond bespoke geospatial services, Consortis Geospatial maintains a strong
product portfolio that includes Evrymap for geoportals and geospatial data
sharing, Landify for property management, MAPtheYA for water networks
management, and the Mobi suite (Road, City, Park, Industry) for inspections,
asset and fault management. These COTS platforms are configurable, scalable,
and interoperable, enabling utilities, municipalities, infrastructure
operators, industrial organizations, and other asset-intensive entities to
accelerate deployment and improve day-to-day operations.

With proven experience across both the public and private sectors, Consortis
Geospatial operates not only as a trusted geospatial services provider, but
also as a product company delivering end-to-end solutions, from data capture
and application development to operational intelligence, informed
decision-making, and the optimization of infrastructure maintenance and
performance.

Finally, Consortis Geospatial is the official distributor of PASCO Corporation
in Greece, a leading Japan-based provider of cutting-edge geospatial products
and services.

PASCO provides access to world-class data and imagery from Japan's advanced
ALOS-1 and ALOS-2 Earth observation satellites, which are equipped with
high-resolution sensors. PASCO's products deliver solutions across a wide range
of sectors, including surface monitoring, disaster management, and
environmental monitoring.
