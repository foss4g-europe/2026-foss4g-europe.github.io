GeoSolutions is the team behind the open-source geospatial stack used by
hundreds of organizations worldwide. Founded in 2006, with offices in Italy
and the United States, we build, maintain, and evolve the software that powers
enterprise-grade Spatial Data Infrastructures across governments, research
institutions, and private industry.

Our staff includes core developers and steering committee members with commit
rights on leading open-source geospatial projects, including GeoServer,
MapStore, GeoNode, GeoNetwork, GeoWebCache, GeoTools, and ImageIO-Ext. Recent
investments include the ongoing modernization toward GeoServer 3, regular
MapStore feature releases, and advanced raster engineering such as native GEOS
projection support for EUMETSAT's meteorological satellite imagery viewer.

We deliver Enterprise Support, Subscription, Professional Training, and custom
development services to mission-critical platforms at organizations such as
EMSA, EUMETSAT, DLR, NOAA, alongside more than 300 other government agencies,
international organizations, and private companies.
