📣 **FOSS4G Europe 2026 has an app!**

📱 Our silver sponsors, [OPENGIS.ch](https://opengis.ch/), have generously set up a [QFieldCloud](https://qfield.cloud/) project to host our conference app, so you can:

- search through the conference schedule;
- select the rooms that host your talks, workshops, and events;
- navigate the event locations;
- explore the conference area;
- keep track of your memories: notes, photos, videos, audio — all in one place.

[QFieldCloud](https://qfield.org/get) is a mobile app for field data collection and editing, built on QGIS. It allows access to QGIS projects and data on mobile devices, enabling work in the field without the need for an internet connection.

1. Download [QFieldCloud](https://qfield.org/get) on your mobile device.
2. Scan the QR code below or follow [https://onelink.to/foss4geu26](https://onelink.to/foss4geu26).
